wPayment terms and conditions - Fees for purchased services

1. Subscription terms

Material Manager offers a range of subscription plans. Each plan includes a certain number of users and features. You will select your plan as part of your registration, which may begin with a free trial. You may be billed monthly, semi annually or annually as per your chosen cycle.

2. Free Trial period

2.1 During the registration process, you get a one time free trial to try out the Material Manager platform from the date you register without paying in advance.
2.2 You can enjoy a free trial of the platform for a period of 30 days, post which the free trial ends and you may have to subscribe to any one of the plans to continue using the platform

3. Payment terms, Refunds, Upgrades

3.1 The fees of your plan are billed in advance, non-refundable and automatically renew on a monthly, semi-annual or annual basis as described in your plan.
3.2 If you upgrade your plan, your credit card will be charged immediately on a prorated amount reflecting the increased rate. You will be charged the full amount of the new rate as part of your new billing cyclebr
3.3 Any discounts applied to the previous subscription may not be applied to the renewed subscription.
3.4 You may also add users over and above the number mentioned as part of each plan. You will be charged an amount per user. If you add more users in the middle of the month/cycle, it would be considered as a new subscription and the amount remaining as part of the previous subscription will be adjusted in the next month.

4. Subscription cancellation

4.1 The only method to cancel your subscription is doing so using the ‘Cancel’ option in the platform.
4.2 If you cancel your plan before the end of your pre-paid period, you will not be charged again for your plan beginning with the next billing cycle. If you cancel your plan, you will lose access to the data on the platform upon expiration of your current pre-paid period.

5. Payment failure

5.1 If you fail to make payments on or before the due date (case of dishonored auto debit ), your subscription will be paused immediately. You will regain access to the platform once the payment is made.
